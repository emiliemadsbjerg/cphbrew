New Brew!

Say hello to summer!
SUMMER IPA is our newest brew, perfect for the hot summer weather. SUMMER IPA is a refreshing Indian Pale Ale, brewed on Cascade and El Dorado Hops, and with a twist of sour lime.
This IPA, is so good, that you will need more than one, once you are done with the first.

Happy drinking!

How do I order CPH Brewhouse Beer?

Since we are a microbrewery still, and we don't have mass production, it is not an option for us to make a webshop.

So in order for you to order beer from us, simply give us a call or send us an email, with how many, and which beer you want.
