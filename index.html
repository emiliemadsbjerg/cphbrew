INTRODUCING YOUBREW!

What is YOUBREW ?

YOUBREW is our newly founded concept, where you can create your own beer with us. We bring the brewery, the ingredients and the rest, while you bring your wishes, ideas and creativity. We strive to create an amazing experience for you and for you to make a memory in form of your very own beer.

Our options

We offer different options for our YOUBREW concept. First of all, it is up to you wether you want to brew on your own, or if you want to invite some of your friends.

Our second offer is to use your beer, for a limited time, in our beer collection. This will cost extra, but you will have your very own limited edition beer, sold as a collaboration with us, which you can brag to your friends about.
